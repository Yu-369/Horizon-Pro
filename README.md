# YouTube Pro

Experience YouTube with a stunning cinematic redesign. YouTube Pro transforms the standard interface into a premium, focused, and ultra-modern experience by stripping away algorithmic noise and clutter.

![Hero Banner](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7d20b5eb-f032-43f6-88f4-d2d2fa93e266)

### 🛠 Full Feature List
- **YouTube Pro Core**
  - **Refined Carousel**: Cinematic hero section on the homepage (ON by default).
  - **3 Videos Per Row**: Toggle between 3 or 4 videos in the home grid for better visibility.
- **Video Sidebar**
  - **Hide Recommended**: Removes the related videos sidebar on watch pages.
  - **Hide Live Chat**: Removes the live chat panel during streams.
  - **Hide Playlist**: Removes the playlist panel from the sidebar.
- **Shorts & End Screens**
  - **Hide YouTube Shorts**: Nukes Shorts shelves, tabs, and grid items (ON by default).
  - **Hide End Screen Videowall**: Removes the grid of videos shown at the end of playback.
  - **Hide End Screen Cards**: Removes in-video cards shown during playback.
- **Comments & Social**
  - **Hide Comments**: Removes the entire comment section.
  - **Hide Mix & Radio Playlists**: Aggressively targets and hides auto-generated mixes (ON by default).
  - **Hide Merch, Tickets, Offers**: Removes promotional shopping shelves (ON by default).
- **Video Metadata**
  - **Hide Video Buttons Bar**: Removes Like, Dislike, Share, and Save buttons.
  - **Hide Channel Info**: Removes the channel avatar and subscribe button.
  - **Hide Video Description**: Removes the description box below the title.
- **Header & Navigation**
  - **Hide Top Header**: Removes the entire masthead bar at the top.
  - **Hide Notification Bell**: Removes the bell icon from the top bar.
  - **Hide "More from YouTube"**: Removes extra links in the sidebar footer.
  - **Hide Playables**: Removes the games shelf and sidebar entry.
- **Smart Search**
  - **Hide Irrelevant Search Results**: Nukes "Related to your search", "Latest from", etc. (ON by default).
- **Playback Controls**
  - **Disable Autoplay**: Prevents next video from auto-playing (ON by default).
  - **Disable Annotations**: Hides in-video annotation overlays and i-cards (ON by default).

## Installation (Developer Mode)

1. **Download**: Grab the latest release ZIP [here](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/27355650/YouTube.Pro.zip).
2. **Extract**: Unzip the folder to a permanent location.
3. **Chrome Extensions**: Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
4. **Developer Mode**: Enable the toggle in the top right.
5. **Load Unpacked**: Select the extracted folder.
6. **Refresh**: Visit [YouTube](https://youtube.com) to see the changes.

## Design Philosophy

YouTube Pro is built on the principle of **Intentional Viewing**. We believe the interface should get out of the way of the content. By utilizing modern CSS techniques and subtle JS-driven enhancements, we've created a skin that feels native yet significantly more premium than the standard UI.
